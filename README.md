# daylight
A visual for time zone difference of two cities. Built for people who can't be bothered calculating and understanding time zone abbreviations. 

Daylight is a simple web app I built for myself because I can never be bothered to figure out timezone abbreviations or mentally calculate how many hours ahead or behind someone is. My brain just doesn't work that way lol

I'm in a long-distance relationship and all I want is to see the time zone difference visually. When will we both be awake? At what times do our day times overlap, even if someone else is a day ahead?

So that's exactly what this does! Pick two cities and you get:
- a visual of the sun and moon moving across the sky for each one,
- the timezone gap between them,
- how many hours of daylight you share,
- and a slider (you can also type it in) to peek forward or back in time.
